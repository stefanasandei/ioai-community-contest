# Responsible AI

Building powerful models is not enough; they should also be fair, robust, interpretable, and safe.

## Topics

- **Fairness:** identifying and mitigating bias in data and models.
- **Interpretability:** understanding why a model made a prediction.
- **Robustness:** handling adversarial examples and distribution shift.
- **Privacy and safety:** protecting data and preventing misuse.

## Why it matters for IOAI

Many AI competitions include evaluation beyond accuracy. Understanding these dimensions helps you build more trustworthy systems and can protect you from hidden test-set surprises.
