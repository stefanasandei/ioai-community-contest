# Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement learning trains agents to make sequences of decisions by rewarding desired behavior.

## Core concepts

- **Agent, environment, states, actions, rewards.**
- **Policy:** the agent's strategy.
- **Value functions:** estimating future rewards.
- **Q-learning and policy gradients.**

## Modern RL

Deep RL combines neural networks with RL algorithms. Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) and Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) are widely used.

## When it appears

RL appears in control, game-playing, and some advanced AI benchmarks. It is less common in classical supervised IOAI tasks but worth knowing.
