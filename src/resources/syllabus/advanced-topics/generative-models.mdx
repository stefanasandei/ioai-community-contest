# Generative Models

Generative models learn to produce new data similar to a training distribution. They power image generation, text generation, music synthesis, and more.

## Families of models

- **VAEs:** probabilistic latent variable models.
- **GANs:** adversarial training between generator and discriminator.
- **Diffusion models:** iterative denoising for high-quality generation.
- **Autoregressive models:** predict tokens one at a time.

## Practical focus

For most applications, start with pre-trained diffusion models or autoregressive LLMs. Train your own only when you have a clear reason and sufficient data.
