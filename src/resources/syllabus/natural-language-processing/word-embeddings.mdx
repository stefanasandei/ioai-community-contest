# Word Embeddings

Word embeddings map words to dense vectors where similar words are close together. They are a bridge between discrete text and continuous mathematics.

## Key methods

- **Word2Vec:** predicting context words or target words.
- **GloVe:** global word-word co-occurrence statistics.
- **FastText:** subword-aware embeddings for rare words.

## Contextual embeddings

Modern embeddings like ELMo, BERT, and GPT produce different vectors for the same word depending on context. This is the foundation of modern NLP.
