# Audio & Signal Processing

Audio tasks require understanding how sound is represented and processed. This section covers signal fundamentals and modern deep learning approaches.

## What you'll learn

- **Audio Basics:** waveforms, sampling, Fourier transforms, and spectrograms.
- **Speech Recognition:** converting speech to text.
- **Audio Classification:** recognizing sounds, music, and speech.

## Approach

Audio data is often converted into spectrograms and treated similarly to images. Learn the signal basics first, then apply CNNs and audio transformers.
