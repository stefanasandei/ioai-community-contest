# Advanced Topics & AI Systems

Once you have a foundation in ML, deep learning, and the major modalities, you can explore advanced topics that increasingly appear in modern AI benchmarks.

## Subsections

- **Reinforcement Learning:** training agents through rewards.
- **Generative Models:** VAEs, GANs, and diffusion models.
- **Multimodal Models:** combining text, images, and audio.
- **Responsible AI:** fairness, robustness, and safe deployment.

## When to study these

Focus on the core sections first. Return here once you can comfortably build and evaluate standard supervised models.
