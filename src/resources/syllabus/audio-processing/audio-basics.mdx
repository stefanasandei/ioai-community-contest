# Audio Basics

Audio is a one-dimensional signal representing pressure variations over time. Before applying deep learning, you need to understand how it is represented.

## Key concepts

- **Sampling rate:** how many samples per second.
- **Amplitude and dynamic range.**
- **Fourier transform:** decomposing a signal into frequencies.
- **Spectrogram:** a time-frequency representation.
- **Mel-spectrogram and MFCCs:** perceptually motivated features.

## Tools

`librosa` is the standard Python library for audio analysis. Learn to load audio, compute spectrograms, and visualize them.

> Listen to your audio and look at its spectrogram side by side.
