# Speech Recognition

Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) converts spoken audio into text. Modern systems are almost entirely neural.

## Pipeline

1. Preprocess audio into features.
2. Encode acoustic information with CNNs, RNNs, or transformers.
3. Map acoustic features to text with CTC, attention, or transducer loss.

## Pre-trained models

Whisper is an excellent starting point for many ASR tasks. Fine-tune it on your target domain for best results.

## Evaluation

Word Error Rate (WER) is the standard metric. Compare predictions and references carefully to understand error patterns.
