# Audio Classification

Audio classification assigns a label to an audio clip. Applications include speech emotion recognition, music genre classification, and environmental sound detection.

## Approaches

- **Hand-crafted features + classical ML:** MFCCs with random forests or SVMs.
- **Spectrogram + CNN:** treat audio as images.
- **Pre-trained audio transformers:** Wav2Vec 2.0, AST, BEATs.

## Data augmentation

- Time stretching and pitch shifting
- Adding background noise
- SpecAugment: masking time and frequency bands

Start with a CNN on mel-spectrograms, then upgrade to transformers if you have enough data.
