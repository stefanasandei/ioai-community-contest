# Sequence Models

Before transformers, RNNs and their variants were the standard for sequential data. They remain useful for understanding how deep learning handles order.

## Architectures

- **RNNs:** the simplest recurrent architecture.
- **LSTMs and GRUs:** gated variants that mitigate vanishing gradients.
- **Bidirectional RNNs:** using future and past context.

## Applications

- Text generation
- Time series forecasting
- Speech recognition preprocessing

## Transition to transformers

Understand the limitations of RNNs—sequential computation and long-range dependency issues—to appreciate why attention was introduced.
