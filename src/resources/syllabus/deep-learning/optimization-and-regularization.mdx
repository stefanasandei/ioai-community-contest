# Optimization & Regularization

Training a neural network is an optimization problem. Regularization helps the model generalize beyond the training set.

## Optimization

- **SGD with momentum:** classic, well-understood optimizer.
- **Adam / AdamW:** adaptive optimizers common in practice.
- **Learning rate schedules:** step decay, cosine annealing, warm restarts.

## Regularization

- **Dropout:** randomly zeroing neurons during training.
- **Weight decay / L2 regularization.**
- **Batch normalization:** stabilizing training and acting as regularization.
- **Data augmentation:** expanding the effective training set.

## Practical advice

Start with AdamW and a simple learning rate schedule. Add regularization when you see overfitting on your validation set.
