# Convolutional Neural Networks

CNNs are the dominant architecture for image and spatial data. They use shared weights and local receptive fields to detect patterns efficiently.

## Core concepts

- **Convolution:** sliding filters across an input.
- **Pooling:** downsampling spatial dimensions.
- **Padding and stride:** controlling output size.
- **Skip connections:** enabling very deep networks.

## Modern architectures

Learn the intuition behind LeNet, AlexNet, VGG, ResNet, and EfficientNet. You don't need to memorize every detail, but understand the design decisions.
