# Foundations of Machine Learning

Before tackling neural networks or transformers, you need a solid grasp of classical machine learning. This section covers the principles that underpin almost every AI system.

## Why this matters

Many IOAI tasks, especially those involving structured data, are best approached with classical methods. Even deep learning tasks benefit from good validation practices and feature engineering.

## Subsections

- **Supervised Learning:** classification and regression from labeled data.
- **Unsupervised Learning:** clustering and dimensionality reduction.
- **Model Evaluation & Validation:** measuring and comparing models fairly.
- **Feature Engineering Basics:** creating and selecting better inputs.

Work through these in order. The habits you build here will carry into every other domain.
