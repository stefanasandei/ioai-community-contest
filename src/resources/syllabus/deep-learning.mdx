# Deep Learning

Deep learning powers the most impressive AI systems today. This section takes you from the basics of neural networks to modern architectures like transformers.

## Learning path

1. **Neural Network Basics:** understand forward passes, backpropagation, and activation functions.
2. **Convolutional Neural Networks:** architectures for spatial data like images.
3. **Sequence Models:** RNNs, LSTMs, and GRUs for sequential data.
4. **Transformers:** attention mechanisms and the architecture behind modern LLMs.
5. **Optimization & Regularization:** how to train models that generalize.

## Connections to other sections

Deep learning is the backbone of the NLP, Computer Vision, and Audio sections. Master this first and the rest becomes much easier.
