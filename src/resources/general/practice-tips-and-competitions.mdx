# Practice Tips & Competitions

Competitions are where theory meets reality. They force you to deal with messy data, limited time, and real evaluation metrics.

## How to practice effectively

1. **Start with guided tutorials.** Kaggle Learn and course notebooks lower the barrier.
2. **Join beginner competitions.** Focus on finishing a full pipeline, not winning.
3. **Read solution write-ups.** The top solutions reveal techniques you may not have considered.
4. **Reproduce winning code.** Type it out and adapt it to understand every detail.
5. **Participate in AICC contests.** They are designed to mirror IOAI-style tasks.

## Building habits

- Keep a notebook of what worked and what did not.
- Build reusable code templates for data loading, training, and inference.
- Set time limits so you don't over-engineer.

> Deliberate practice beats random participation.
